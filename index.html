Does the Internet know more about you than you think it should?

The New Organs is an independent project commissioned by Mozilla to investigate how internet companies track and monitor people online and offline.

We're collecting stories of online surveillance and will be sharing some of our findings later this year. If you've ever had an invasive or creepy experience with online ads, we'd like to hear from you. Fill out the form below, or email us at contact@neworgans.net.

About: This project is made by Tega Brain and is commissioned by Mozilla. To learn more about it, read the project announcement.

All contributions here will be confidential, and handled as described in the Mozilla Privacy Policy. We may follow up with individual contributors to hear more about your experiences.
