Are you a data scientist working in advertising? Tell us about your work.

The New Organs is an independent project commissioned by Mozilla to investigate how internet companies track and monitor people online and offline.

We're collecting stories of how targeted advertising works and will be sharing some of our findings later this year in a series of videos. So far, we’ve investigated common theories of how online ads work, such as the well known “Facebook is listening to me” anecdote.

We’re also collecting stories from data scientists working on advertising technologies. How does data science in advertising work? How do companies use data to target and predict people’s activities?
